About Mile Money

Hi there, thank you for stopping by to check out Mile Money. Mile Money is the result of two things. A innate desire to save every penny I can, and the willingness to learn new cool technologies.

My name is Scott Schubert, I began programming in early 2017 and it has been a revelation for me as it's something I truly enjoy doing. I've also been a runner since doing my first marathon in 2011. For a number of years now I have been either running into work or riding my bike. I even sold my car because I simply was not using it anymore. I had been thinking for a long time that it would be great to be able to visualise how much money I was actually saving by commuting into work by foot or by bike.

I decided to embark on this project to fulfill my vision and also to learn a lot of new things along the way. The project really is the result of my love of running and of programming. I hope this shows through and that you can get some value of the tool.

Feel free to get in contact with me via Twitter, Instagram or Facebook. Get out there and earn some Mile Money!
